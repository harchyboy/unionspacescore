All-Inclusive Scope

Fixed at signing

Services included in landlord bundle are fixed for lease duration

Cannot reduce bundled services without lease modification

Quantity adjustments may be possible within service type

Annual review allows for scope reassessment

Example: Cannot remove cleaning service from bundle, but can adjust frequency within package tier.

New Service Additions

Direct UNION agreements

Any service added post-signing becomes direct UNION contract

Flexible terms independent of lease structure

Can be modified or cancelled with standard notice

Invoiced separately from bundled services

Example: Adding IT support 6 months after move-in creates new direct agreement with UNION.

Strategic Consideration

Choose initial bundle scope carefully. While additions are straightforward, removing services from All-Inclusive packages requires landlord consent and may impact lease terms. For high-growth companies expecting significant service changes, consider starting with core essentials and adding services as direct agreements to maintain maximum flexibility.
