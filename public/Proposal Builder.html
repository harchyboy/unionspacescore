Proposal Assumptions & Terms

Key conditions and commercial confidence notes

Commercially Confident Pricing

All pricing is final and reflects transparent unit economics. Post-HoT, UNION pricing is rarely renegotiated. This proposal represents our best commercial terms based on the selected services and deal type.

Service Scope & Modifications

For All-Inclusive deals, the service scope is fixed at signing. Any additions or modifications after UNION agreement execution will be handled as separate direct agreements. For Bolt-On deals, services can be added or modified with 30 days notice.

Contract Terms & Commitment

Monthly service costs are fixed for the first 12 months. Annual review applies for subsequent years with 90 days notice. Minimum commitment period aligns with lease term. Early termination subject to 3 months notice and breakage fees.

Installation & Go-Live Timeline

One-time setup costs cover all installation, configuration, and commissioning activities. Lead times vary by service (internet: 6-8 weeks, AV: 2-3 weeks, telephony: 2 weeks). Full coordination timeline provided upon acceptance.

Landlord Appetite & Variability

For All-Inclusive deals, landlord appetite and service variability (e.g., coffee consumption, AV specialty requirements) determine what remains bundled vs. moves to direct UNION contracts. Our recommendation reflects optimal commercial structure for this property.

Service Level Commitments

All services include defined SLAs with response and resolution times. Critical services (internet, security) include 24/7 support. Detailed SLA documentation provided in service agreements. Performance reporting available monthly.

Proposal Validity

This proposal is valid for 30 days from generation date. Pricing and service availability are subject to change after this period. Upon acceptance, this proposal forms the basis of the formal UNION Service Agreement.
