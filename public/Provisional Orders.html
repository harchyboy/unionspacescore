Break Clause Option

Added to standard no-break lease

Agreed
Standard Terms

5-year term with no break clause provisions

Negotiated Exception

Tenant break option at Year 3.5 with 9 months written notice. Conditional on tenant being up-to-date with all rent and service charge payments. Provides tenant flexibility while protecting landlord's minimum term security.

Logged: 11 Nov 2024 by Max Sullivan

Service Charge Cap

Annual increase limitation added

Agreed
Standard Terms

Service charge passed through at cost with no annual cap

Negotiated Exception

Maximum 5% annual increase on service charge unless due to statutory requirements or emergency works. Protects tenant from unexpected cost escalation while maintaining landlord's ability to recover genuine increased costs.

Logged: 11 Nov 2024 by Max Sullivan
