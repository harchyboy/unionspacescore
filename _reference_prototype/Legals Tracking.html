Deal Context & Notes

Background and negotiation history

Negotiation Summary

Initial terms proposed at £65 psf with 1 month rent free. After three rounds of negotiation, final agreement reached at £60 psf with 3 months rent free and break clause at month 36. Landlord was motivated to secure quality tenant for long-term occupancy. Break clause was key tenant requirement for flexibility.

All-Inclusive Deal Structure

This is an All-Inclusive arrangement where landlord has appetite for bundled services. UNION maintains separate service agreement with landlord for core services (cleaning, security, waste management). Additional tenant-specific services (coffee, AV, plants) are direct UNION agreements. This structure provides landlord with predictable service delivery while allowing tenant flexibility for add-ons.

Special Provisions

Landlord agreed to Cat A fit-out prior to commencement, which accelerates tenant move-in timeline. 24/7 access granted with building notification requirement. Tenant may engage third-party service providers subject to building regulations—this clause was critical for UNION's service model and was explicitly negotiated to ensure flexibility.

4 parking spaces included in base rent; additional spaces available at £200/month each. Tenant expressed interest in potential expansion to adjacent floor (Suite 602) in approximately 18 months if business growth continues.

Post-Agreement Actions

Landlord to complete Cat A works by 20 December 2024. Schedule of Condition to be agreed by both parties before commencement. Rent deposit of £93,000 (3 months) to be held in designated account. Legal documentation to be executed by 30 November 2024. First rent payment due 15 April 2025 (after 3-month rent free period).
